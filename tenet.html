Tenet
TENET is a 2020 science fiction action film directed by Christopher Nolan. The film follows a secret agent who is tasked with preventing World War III through the use of time-inverting technology.

TENET is a visually stunning film with high-quality action sequences and an engaging, complex plot that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats. The film's cast delivers strong performances and using time manipulation as a central plot element adds a unique twist to the traditional espionage genre. Overall, TENET is a thrilling and well-crafted film that will appeal to fans of action and science fiction.

The film stars:

John David Washington

Robert Pattinson

Elizabeth Debicki

Dimple Kapadia

Kenneth Branagh
