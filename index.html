Who is he?

Christopher Nolan is a British-American film director, screenwriter, and producer. He is best known for his work on the Dark Knight trilogy, Inception, Interstellar, and Dunkirk, among many other critically acclaimed and commercially successful films. Nolan has been nominated for numerous awards throughout his career, including Academy Awards for Best Original Screenplay and Best Director.

Why is he my favorite?

Christopher Nolan is a filmmaking genius because of his ability to craft complex, thought-provoking narratives that engage and entertain audiences. His films often have multiple layers of meaning and symbolism, and he is skilled at using a variety of techniques, such as nonlinear storytelling, to keep viewers engaged and invested in the story. Additionally, Nolan is known for his attention to detail and his willingness to take creative risks, which have resulted in some of the most memorable and innovative films of the past few decades.
