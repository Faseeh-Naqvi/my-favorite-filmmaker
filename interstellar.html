Interstellar

Interstellar is a science fiction film about a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity. The film explores themes of love, loss, and the human drive to survive against all odds.
   

Interstellar boasts stunning visual effects that transport the viewer to a world beyond our own. The film's depiction of space travel, black holes, and otherworldly landscapes is a feast for the eyes that will leave audiences in awe. Fun fact, this black hole image is scientifically acurate! The light you see above and below the black hole is actually behind it, because of the black holes ability to bend light, they look like this!

 Starring:

Matthew McConaughey

Anne Hathaway

Michael Caine
